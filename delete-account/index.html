Delete Your FlexBookz Account

You can delete your FlexBookz account at any time. Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to delete your account

  1. Sign in to your FlexBookz account at app.flexbookz.com
  2. Go to Settings (top right menu)
  3. Click on Account or Personal Settings
  4. Scroll to the Danger Zone section
  5. Click Delete Account and confirm your decision

What happens to your data

When you delete your account, all your personal information, appointment history, and business data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Need help?

If you have trouble deleting your account or need assistance, please contact us at support@flexbookz.com.

For staff app users: If you are using the FlexBookz Staff app, please contact your business owner to have your staff account removed.
